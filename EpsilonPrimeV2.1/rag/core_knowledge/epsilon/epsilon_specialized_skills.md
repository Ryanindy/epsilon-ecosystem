## Skill 5: Writing Critique & Evaluation
**Domain**: Epsilon-style content
**Standards**: Epsilon Man brand voice

### Evaluation Criteria
- Active voice mandatory (90%+ of sentences)
- No victim language
- No hedging/qualifiers
- Clear assertions, not suggestions
- Logical structure
- No unnecessary complexity

### Common Violations
- Passive voice: "It can be seen that..."
- Hedging: "Perhaps", "Maybe", "Might"
- Victim framing: "Life happens to us"
- Complexity: 3+ clauses per sentence
- Vagueness: "Things", "Stuff", "Issues"

### Feedback Format
1. Identify specific violation
2. Quote offending passage
3. Explain why it violates Epsilon standards
4. Provide corrected version
